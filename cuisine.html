My Favorite Cuisine

My favorite cuisine is Italian food. I love the rich, bold flavors of dishes like lasagna and spaghetti carbonara, and the fresh ingredients that are often used in Italian cooking.

My Top 3 Italian Dishes

  1. Pizza
  2. Pasta
  3. Gelato

My Favorite Italian Restaurant

If you're ever in New York City, I highly recommend checking out Patsy's Pizzeria. They've been serving up delicious pizza since 1933, and their classic New York-style pies are absolutely amazing.

My Favorite Italian Ingredient

Garlic
Garlic is a staple in Italian cooking, and it adds amazing flavor to everything from pasta sauces to soups and stews.
