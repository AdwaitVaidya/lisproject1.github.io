My Favorite Book

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

The Alchemist is a novel by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho that was first published in 1988. It tells the story of Santiago, an Andalusian shepherd boy who yearns to travel in search of a worldly treasure as extravagant as any ever found. He sets off on a journey to fulfill his destiny, encountering a series of characters along the way who teach him valuable life lessons.

  • The Alchemist has been translated into 80 languages.
  • It has sold over 65 million copies worldwide, making it one of the best-selling books in history.
  • The book has been praised by numerous famous figures, including Oprah Winfrey and Will Smith.
